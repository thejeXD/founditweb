Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Quick FAQ Summary:
  • How to Report: Use the Submit Item page to report lost or found items.
  • Claiming Items: Click on an item and use the Claim button if it's yours.
  • Notifications: Check your Inbox for updates and claims.
  • Privacy: Your data is protected and only used for item recovery.
  • Eligibility: Only students, staff, and faculty can use FoundIT.
What is FoundIT?▶
FoundIT is a campus-wide lost and found system that helps students and staff report, track, and recover lost or found items efficiently and securely.
How do I report a lost or found item?▶
Log in to your FoundIT account and click on "Submit Item" in the sidebar. Fill out the form with the item details and submit. Your report will be reviewed and listed in the system.
Can I claim an item I found in the system?▶
Yes. If you see your lost item listed, click on it and use the "Claim" button. The owner or admin will review your claim and contact you for verification.
How do I know if my lost item has been found?▶
You will receive a notification in your FoundIT inbox if someone reports or claims an item matching your description. You can also check the "Inbox" section for updates.
Is my personal information safe?▶
Yes. FoundIT values your privacy. Your information is only used for item recovery and is not shared with third parties. Please see our EULA for more details.
What if I submitted incorrect information?▶
You can edit or update your item details from the "My Items" section. If you need further assistance, contact the campus Lost & Found office.
Who can use FoundIT?▶
All students, staff, and faculty with a valid campus account can use FoundIT to report, search, and claim lost or found items.
How long are items kept in the system?▶
Items are listed for up to 2 months from the date reported. After that, they may be archived or removed according to campus policy.
