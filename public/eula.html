End User License Agreement

Key Points Summary:

  • Student Verification: You must be a registered student with valid credentials to use the system
  • Accurate Reporting: All lost and found item reports must be accurate and include clear descriptions
  • Safe Returns: Users are responsible for arranging safe item pickup/return
  • Campus Only: The system is for campus-related items only
  • Zero Tolerance: False reports or misuse will result in account termination

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing and using the FoundIT lost and found system, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these terms. This service is exclusively for students and staff of our institution.

2. User Eligibility and Registration

To use FoundIT, you must:

  • Enrollment: Be a currently enrolled student or active staff member
  • Credentials: Register using your valid institutional credentials
  • Profile: Maintain an up-to-date profile with accurate contact information
  • Age: Be at least 13 years of age

3. Item Reporting Guidelines

When reporting lost or found items, you agree to:

  • Description: Provide accurate and detailed item descriptions
  • Documentation: Include clear photos of found items when possible
  • Location: Report only items lost or found on campus premises
  • Details: Specify accurate location and time details
  • Updates: Update the status of your reports promptly

4. User Responsibilities

As a FoundIT user, you are responsible for:

  • Safety: Arranging safe meetups for item returns in designated campus areas
  • Verification: Verifying item ownership before returning items
  • Security: Maintaining confidentiality of your account credentials
  • Reporting: Reporting any suspicious activity or misuse
  • Compliance: Following campus security guidelines during item exchanges

5. Privacy and Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is:

  • Usage: Used only for lost and found system operations
  • Sharing: Shared only with relevant campus security when necessary
  • Storage: Stored securely and handled according to institutional policies
  • Retention: Retained only for the duration necessary for service provision

6. System Limitations and Disclaimers

Please understand that FoundIT:

  • Recovery: Does not guarantee the recovery of lost items
  • Condition: Is not responsible for item condition or authenticity
  • Verification: Cannot verify all user-submitted information
  • Availability: May experience occasional maintenance downtimes

7. Account Termination

Your account may be terminated for:

  • False Claims: Submitting false reports or claims
  • Misuse: Misusing the system for non-lost-and-found purposes
  • Violations: Violating campus safety guidelines during item exchanges
  • Harassment: Harassing other users or staff
  • Status: No longer being an active student or staff member
